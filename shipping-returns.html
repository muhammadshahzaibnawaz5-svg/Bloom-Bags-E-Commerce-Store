Shipping Policy

At Blome, we believe your order should arrive as beautifully as it was crafted. We partner with premium carriers to ensure every package is handled with care from our atelier to your doorstep.

Free Express Shipping on all domestic orders over $200. Standard shipping is a flat $8.95 for orders under $200.

Domestic Shipping (USA)

  • Standard (5–8 business days) — $8.95
  • Express (2–3 business days) — $14.95
  • Free Express — on orders $200+

International Shipping

  • Standard (10–18 business days) — $25.00
  • Express (5–9 business days) — $45.00
  • Free Express on international orders over $350

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed; Blome is not responsible for carrier delays or customs processing times.

International orders may be subject to import duties, taxes, and customs clearance fees levied by the destination country. These charges are the buyer's responsibility and are not included in the purchase price or shipping cost.

Returns & Exchanges

Your satisfaction is our commitment. If you are not completely in love with your Blome handbag, we are here to make it right.

30-Day Easy Returns. Items may be returned or exchanged within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or store credit.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.
  • Footwear, jewelry, and accessories must be returned in their original boxes.
  • Final sale items, including clearance and personalized products, are not eligible for return.
  • We reserve the right to refuse returns that show signs of wear, damage, or misuse.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Log in to your account dashboard and select the order you wish to return.
  • Click "Start Return" and follow the prompts. A prepaid return shipping label will be generated.
  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging, attach the label, and drop it off at any carrier location.
  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the item. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

For exchanges, select your desired replacement during the return process. Exchanges ship free of charge within the domestic USA.
